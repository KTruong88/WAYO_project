WAYŌ means
harmony in Japanese

The experience

Each night at WAYŌ we combine technique, tradition, and evolution to
transform ten seats into memorable omakase experiences. Our highly
skilled chefs meticulously craft and guide our guests through each course
allowing everyone an opportunity to delve into the intricacies of each dish.

Our philosophy

Inspired by sushi bars and the Southern California lifestyle,
Michelin Star Chef Hisashi Miyamoto created WAYŌ to craft
an elevated sushi experience in an atmosphere that is both
intimate and approachable. Carefully curated Japanese
shochu or local craft beer accompanies each course. This
harmonious pairing of food with drinks is well suited for our
name, which is based on the Japanese concepts of wayo
setchu, meaning “a blending of Japanese and Western styles
and concepts” and wa, translating to both “harmony” and
“gentleness of spirit” implying a peaceful unity.

Our fish

At the core of any great sushi restaurant is the fish. Our
tasting menu showcases traditional, exquisite fare using
the finest ingredients and the freshest, highest quality fish
imported three times per week directly from the world-
renowned Tsukiji Fish Market in Tokyo. Having developed
strong bonds with various vendors over the years, we can
say without a doubt that the fish on our plates is quite
literally the finest available anywhere in the world.

Our chef

Born and raised in Tokyo, Executive Chef Hisashi Miyamoto has been preparing traditional
Edomae sushi for nearly 20 years. After moving to San Diego at the age of 18, Chef Miyamoto
took the time to hone his technical skills and cultivate his knowledge of traditional cuisine at
San Diego’s most highly respected Japanese restaurants, before opening WAYŌ in 2016.

To ensure WAYŌ’s sushi remains at the peak of the industry, Chef Miyamoto frequently
embarks on culinary research trips annually, tasting the best omakase and Edomae sushi in
major destinations around the world. Additionally, Chef Miyamoto regularly travels to Japan
to gain inspiration and stage at the top local restaurants to gain first-hand expert knowledge
of the latest trends. This attention to detail influences WAYŌ’s style, menu and guest
experience, assuring it remains a top sushi destination.

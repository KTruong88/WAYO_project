Reservation required

WAYŌ offers two seatings each evening, at 6 pm and 8 pm.

We are an intimate restaurant with only 10 seats. Last-minute
cancellations or changes in party size impact us significantly. In
order to stay true to the experience we provide every guest, we
require a credit card number to hold reservations and enforce a
strict cancellation policy.

Cancellations or changes in party size made with less than 48 hours
(2 days) notice before your reservation will incur a $120 per person
charge. Thank you in advance for your patience and understanding.
We look forward to seeing you soon.

Menu

WAYŌ offers an 18-course omakase menu with the opportunity to
indulge in extra courses. Our menu changes in harmony with nature
as we seek to reveal the essence of each ingredient at its peak.
Every ingredient is meticulously sourced from the best purveyors in
the world; about 90% of our fish comes from the famous Tsukiji
Market in Tokyo, with the rest coming from specialty purveyors.

Each course is a complete composition woven into a menu telling a
unique story through technique, tradition, and evolution. We offer a
curated selection of fine shōchū and craft beer to complement your
unique omakase experience. Our knowledgeable servers can suggest
pairings throughout the entire menu.
